## Ingredients
(Halve the ingredients below if making in a clay pot)

- 4 chicken thighs, cut into bite size pieces
- 4 Chinese sausage/腊肠, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 thumb of ginger, minced
- 2 cups jasmine rice, washed
- 1/2 cup dried black fungus/木耳, soaked and sliced
- 6 shiitake mushrooms, sliced (can substitute dried)
- 6-8 heads of bok choy, washed and separated
- Sauce:
    - 2 tbsp oyster sauce
    - 4 tbsp soy sauce
    - 1 tbsp rice wine
    - 1 tbsp dark soy
    - 2 tsp sugar
    - 2 tsp sesame oil

## Instructions
1. Heat up oil in Dutch oven over medium heat
1. Brown chicken thighs and Chinese sausage
1. Add garlic and ginger, continue sautéing until fragrant
1. Add rice, black fungus, mushrooms, and half of the sauce and mix
1. Place bok choy on top of rice mixture and add water to cover
1. Turn heat to high
1. Once simmering, turn down heat and cover for 20 minutes 
1. Turn off heat and let stand for 5-10 minutes
1. Mix in remaining sauce
1. Pour small amount of oil along walls of Dutch oven
1. Turn heat to high for 5 minutes
