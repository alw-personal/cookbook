## Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup ripe bananas mashed (about 3)
- 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup butter (melted)

## Steps
1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately, then combine
3. Bake for 50 minutes at 180c
