## Ingredients
- 3 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup butter (melted)
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup pumpkin seeds
- 1/2 cup chocolate chips

## Steps
1. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately, then combine
2. Fold in toppings
2. Roll dough into ping-pong size balls
3. Bake for 10 minutes at 175c
