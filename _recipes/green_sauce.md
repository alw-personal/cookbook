## Ingredients
- 1 bunch cilantro
- 1 bunch green onions
- 2 jalapenos
- 1 habanero
- 1 lime
- 1 avocado
- 1/4 cup neutral oil
- 1/4 cup mayo
- pinch salt

## Steps
- Combine everything in food processor
