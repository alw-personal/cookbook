## Ingredients
- 3.5 lb pork neck bones
- 6 chicken thigh bones
- Pork fat (cut off of pork shoulder)
- 4 cloves garlic
- 3 green onions
- 1 apple

## Equipment
- Instant Pot
- Immersion blender

## Instructions
1. Soak pork neck bones over night
1. Rinse and scrub
1. Boil in stock pot until no more brown scum
1. Rinse and scrub
1. Add pork neck bones and chicken thigh bones to instant pot with enough water to cover by an inch
1. Pressure cook on high for 8 hour
1. Natural release
1. Add one liter of water, pork fat, garlic, onion, apple
1. Cook at a rolling boil for another hour
1. Strain out all of the solids
1. Immersion blend
