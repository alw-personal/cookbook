## Ingredients
- 1 kg pork shoulder
- 1 bottle Lee Kum Kee Char Siu Sauce
- 1 tsp dark soy

## Steps
- Marinate for 4 hours
- Roast at 220c for 20 minutes each side

## Notes
- Can substitute chicken
