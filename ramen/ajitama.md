# Ajitama

## Ingredients
- 5 eggs
- 500 ml water
- 100 ml shoyu
- 100 ml mirin

## Steps
1. Softboil eggs, ice bath, peel
1. Heat brine until simmering
1. Cure eggs in brine for 2 days

## Notes
- 3/4/21: too salty, halved shoyu (200 ml → 100 ml)
