# Beef udon
---
Serves: ??  
Time: ??

## Ingredients

### For udon broth
- 2.5 cups Dashi
- 1.5 Tbsp soy
- 1 Tbsp mirin
- 1 tsp sugar
- salt

### For udon noodle soup
- 2 servings udon noodles
- 8 oz thinly sliced beef
- 2 green onions (slice green and white parts separately)
- 1 Tbsp soy
- 2 tsp ugar

## Instructions

### Udon broth
1. Prepare dashi broth
2. Add soy, miring, sugar, and salt (to taste) to dashi broth and remove from heat

### Beef
1. Stir fry green onion in vegetable oil
2. Add beef
2. Add sugar and soy, coat

## Udon noodle soup
1. Cook udon noodles and drain
1. Assemble
